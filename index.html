About CCCGJ 关于杰克逊华人基督教会

Founded in 2007 by a group of Chinese immigrant Christians, CCCGJ is a Baptist church rooted in the gospel of Jesus Christ. While our name includes the word “Chinese,” we warmly welcome people of all ethnic backgrounds. At CCCGJ, both English- and Mandarin-speaking individuals hear the same gospel, grow together in Christ, and serve in God’s kingdom.
CCCGJ（杰克逊华人基督教会）由一群华人移民基督徒于2007年创立，是一间扎根于福音的浸信会教会。虽然教会的名字中包含“华人”一词，但我们热情欢迎来自各种不同族裔背景的人。在 CCCGJ，讲英文和讲普通话的人都能听到同一个福音，在基督里一同成长，并在神的国度中参与服侍。
