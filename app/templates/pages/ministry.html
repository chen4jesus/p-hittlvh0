事工活动

Worship Service 主日敬拜

9:30am - 11:00am (bilingual in English and Mandarin 中英文双语)

主日敬拜期间，家长带领孩子一同敬拜。在敬拜大厅后方有一个儿童和婴孩室，供不能坚持长时间敬拜的孩子和家庭。

Parents and their Children worship together. There is a children room and nursery room for the kids and the families who cannot stay in the Sanctuary for a long time.

Gospel Fellowship 福音团契

2nd & 4th Fridays (Monthly)

每个月的第二个周五和第四个周五的晚上，分别在Madison和Clinton弟兄姊妹的家中聚会，特别为平时不来教会的朋友准备。

On the second Friday evening of each month, we gather at the home of brothers and sisters in Madison; on the fourth Friday evening, we meet at the home of brothers and sisters in Clinton. These gatherings are especially arranged for friends who don’t usually attend church.
